Dr. (H.C.) Ir. H. Soekarno

The leader of the Indonesian struggle

Soekarno was an Indonesian statesman, orator, revolutionary, and nationalist who was the first president of Indonesia, serving from 1945 to 1967. Soekarno was the leader of the Indonesian struggle for independence from the Dutch colonialists.
